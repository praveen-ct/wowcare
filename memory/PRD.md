# Wow Care Family Salon - Website PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Build a modern, elegant, and professional business website for "Wow Care Family Salon" - a women-owned beauty parlour in Basavanagudi, Bengaluru. The website should showcase services, customer reviews, location details, and provide easy booking options through phone and WhatsApp.

## Business Details
- **Business Name**: Wow Care Family Salon
- **Category**: Beauty Parlour / Family Salon
- **Rating**: ⭐ 4.9/5 (91 reviews)
- **Tagline**: Professional Care. Beautiful Results.
- **Location**: Basavanagudi, Bengaluru, Karnataka - 560004
- **Phone**: +91 93413 43216
- **Working Hours**: Open Daily, Closes at 10 PM

## User Personas
1. **Local Residents**: Women and families looking for reliable, affordable beauty services
2. **Bridal Clients**: Brides seeking professional makeup and pre-bridal packages
3. **Regular Customers**: Clients looking for monthly packages and consistent quality
4. **First-time Visitors**: People discovering the salon through online search

## Core Requirements (Static)
1. Modern, elegant, and professional design with rose gold, beige, white, and black colors
2. Fully responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop
3. Complete information architecture with 6 main pages
4. Easy booking through phone and WhatsApp
5. Google Maps integration for location
6. High-quality salon imagery throughout
7. Customer testimonials showcase
8. SEO-friendly content structure
